MKRS MRGR

Connect Wallet

Use the MKRS MRGR to combine your MKRS into your ideal PFP. Pick and choose the traits you want to keep between two of your MKRS. The MRGR makes the MKRS deflationary by burning one of the MKRS while applying the desired attributes to the other one. The XP of the two MKRS will be combined making the resulting MKRS more valuable.
